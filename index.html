Hi! My name is Gerald, a BS-HRM graduate and currently in the process of transitioning into the Tech Industry as a Data Analyst.

My enthusiasm lies in working with data, where I enjoy analyzing it and devising solutions for various problems. Prior to this career shift, I have acquired experience in Data Entry and Virtual Assistance. Additionally, I possess -

• Proficiency in generating data visualizations and conducting analyses using Microsoft Excel.

• PostgreSQL, Power BI, R, and Python.

• Continuously enhancing my capabilities, currently learning advanced SQL language, Tableau, Power BI, R, and Python.

Given my strong statistical foundation and eagerness to acquire new skills and techniques, I have full confidence in my potential to thrive as a data analyst and make a significant impact in this field. - Let me help you make data driven decisions!
